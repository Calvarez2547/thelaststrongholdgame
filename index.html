Story Overview

Three Acts of Resistance, Corruption, and Survival

Based on the uploaded story document, The Last Stronghold frames its conflict around a final defense against a spreading darkness. The setting carries a sense of collapse, corrupted power, and mounting pressure as the last safe point comes under threat.

The player enters that struggle as a guardian figure standing between the obelisk and the enemy waves, fighting to preserve the stronghold while the danger escalates across the campaign.

The overall arc supports a dramatic capstone tone: a desperate beginning, a growing confrontation, and a last stand built around survival, sacrifice, and control of arcane power.
